What We Use
Premium Materials
Carbon Fiber
Gloss · Matte · Multiple Colors Available

Aerospace-grade carbon fiber with a distinctive 2×2 twill weave. Gloss or matte lacquer, available in black, red, blue, gold, and silver — matched to your Corvette's style.

Nappa Leather
Full-Grain Italian · Smooth or Perforated

Buttery full-grain Nappa leather — the same quality used in high-end European sport cars. Smooth or perforated, with your choice of contrast thread color.

Alcantara®
Genuine Alcantara® · Racing-Grade Suede

The material of choice for Formula 1 steering wheels and exotic interiors. Alcantara® offers superior grip, minimal glare, and an unmatched tactile feel at speed.
