What We Use
Premium Materials
Carbon Fiber
Gloss · Matte · Color Options

Aerospace-grade 2×2 twill weave carbon fiber. Available in gloss or matte lacquer finish and a range of colors — classic black, red, blue, gold tint, and more.

Nappa Leather
Full-Grain Italian · Smooth or Perforated

Buttery full-grain Nappa leather sourced from Italy. The same quality used in European luxury sports cars. Available smooth or perforated with custom thread color choices.

Alcantara®
Genuine · Racing-Grade Suede

The preferred material of Formula 1 teams and exotic car manufacturers. Superior grip, minimal glare, and a premium tactile feel that lasts far longer than natural suede.

About the Process

Every Piece Handmade,
Every Time

We don't use machines for the stitching. Each steering wheel wrap, shift knob, and handbrake grip is cut by hand from full material sheets, wrapped carefully around the original part, and sewn with a hand needle or awl.

Lead time is 10–14 business days because quality takes time. We'd rather take longer and deliver something that lasts years than rush a piece that fails in months.
